About Us

Huda

Huda loves to help people and is working in Community Investment and the Volunteering sector. Huda gained an interest in Code and Tech when she began to teach herself how to code and came across the Code First: Girls crash course. Huda hopes to continue to increase the amount of women in Technology in the next five years and to encourage girls from a young age that computers are for everyone!


#girlsthatcode

Sahra

“My name is Sahra and I’m in my second year of my undergraduate degree. I chose to do Code First: Girls because it was so wildly different from anything I have ever learned before. I heard about the course through a friend and signed up. After this course I hope to use my new coding skills and learn how to create more web pages."

Maya

Maya is a third year Applied Linguistics and French Language BA student at the University of London. She joined the Code First: Girls course after a recommendation from a friend who is now doing the BTFurtHER scheme. Maya hopes to join her language and newfound coding skills to get into Natural Language Processing in the Artificial Intelligence sector. She'd like to teach robots how to speak!
